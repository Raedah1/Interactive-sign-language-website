Choose your dominant hand and BSL preference

Hand preference is important when learning BSL because some signs are performed with one hand while others require the use of both hands. Knowing your hand preference can help you learn the signs more effectively during the lessons by allowing you to focus on the hand movements that are most comfortable and natural for you.
